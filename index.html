Privacy Policy

Lakshya · by Tapas Studio · Last updated: 23 May 2026

Data collection

Lakshya stores all data locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service of any kind.

Personal information

The app does not collect, process, or share any personal information. It does not require an account, sign-in, or internet connection.

Analytics & advertising

The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs of any kind.

Camera & photos

The app requests camera and photo library access solely to let you attach progress photos to your habits. These photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Data deletion

All habit data, settings, and history remain entirely on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data.

Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.
