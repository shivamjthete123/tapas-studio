Privacy Policy

Lakshya · by Tapas Studio · Last updated: 24 May 2026

Overview

Lakshya is a fully offline habit tracker. All data you enter stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted to Tapas Studio, third parties, or any external server.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. The app does not require an account, email address, or any sign-in. All habit data, logs, settings, and progress photos are stored exclusively in local device storage.

Permissions used

  • Notifications — to send you scheduled habit reminders at times you choose. Notification data never leaves your device.
  • Camera & photo library — only to let you attach progress photos to your habits. Photos are stored locally and never uploaded.
  • Boot completed — to reschedule your reminders automatically after the device restarts. No data is read or transmitted.

Analytics & advertising

The app contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising networks. No usage data of any kind is collected or shared.

Third-party services

Lakshya does not integrate with any third-party services, social networks, or cloud platforms.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app permanently and irreversibly deletes all your data from the device. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
